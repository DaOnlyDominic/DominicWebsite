Banana Bread:

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 Cup Butter (I use unsalted)
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 2 Beaten Eggs
  • 4 Finely Crushed Bananas (The browner the better)
  • 1 1/2 Cups Flour
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla

  4. Combine well.

  6. Mix just until combined. Do not overmix.
  7. Pour into greased and floured loaf pan.
  8. Bake at 350ยบ / 180ยบ for 55 minutes.
