For 1 Loaf of Banana Bread:

Ingredients:

  • -1/2 Cup Butter (I use unsalted)
  • -1 Cup Sugar
  • -2 Beaten Eggs
  • -4 Finely Crushed Bananas
  • -1 1/2 Cups Flour
  • -1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • -1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  • -1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla

Directions

  • 1. Preheat oven to 350º.
  • 2. Cream together butter and sugar.
  • 3. Add eggs and crushed bananas.
  • 4. Combine well.
  • 5. Sift together flour, soda and salt. Add to creamed mixture. Add vanilla.
  • 6. Mix just until combined. Do not overmix.
  • 7. Pour into greased and floured loaf pan.
  • 8. Bake at 350 for 55 minutes.

For 2 Loafs of Pumpkin Bread:

Ingredients:

  • -2 Cups Flour
  • -1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • -1/2 Teaspoon Baking Powder
  • -1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  • -Opt: 1 Teaspoon Cloves
  • -Opt: 1 Teaspoon Cinnamon
  • -Opt: 1 Teaspoon Nutmeg
  • -1 Teaspoon Vanilla
  • -3/4 Cup Butter
  • -2 Cups Sugar
  • -2 Eggs
  • -1 15OZ Can of Pumpkin

Directions

  • 1. Preheat oven to 325º, grease 2 loaf pans and dust with flour.
  • 2. In a medium bowl, combine the flour, salt, baking soda, baking powder, cloves, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Whisk until well combined; set aside.
  • 3. Beat the butter and sugar on medium speed until just blended. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Continue beating until very light and fluffy, a few minutes.
  • 4. Beat in the pumpkin.
  • 5. Add the flour mixture and mix.
  • 6. Divide the batter amongst the greased pans.
  • 7. Bake at 325°, for 65-75 minutes.
